Alone in the Unknown

In the quit of this strange place Where shawdows dance upon my face Lie's marathon unfolds at a snails pace No Trust, No refuge, No safe space Zero, nada, echoes in the void Where is our Heru, our savior deployed? Bound by sins, no path to escape Chasing victories, a relentless chase I long to lift my mother from earths embrace This life, a frantic race, a relentless race "NO, NO, NO," I cry, defiance in my tone How can my oppressor grant true education But listen closely, for power awaits When i ascend, I'll reclaim my fate Unseat the tyrant from his lofty throne And cast him down, his bones overthrown For he fed me lies through countless years Yet now i hunger for truth undying and clear
